Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The Heaps Goods Co. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Dual N-Back mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game scores and performance history
  • Levels reached and session history
  • User preferences and settings

You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

b) Third-Party Services

Our App includes third-party SDKs that operate directly on your device. These services may independently collect certain device and usage data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect certain device information, including:

  • Device identifiers (including advertising ID)
  • IP address (full IP address may be shared)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data for ad targeting

Google may use this information to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (e.g., resetting your advertising ID or limiting ad tracking on iOS/Android).

For more information, see Google AdMob's privacy practices and Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. Firebase Analytics collects anonymized and aggregated data, including:

  • App usage patterns (sessions, retention, feature usage)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. For more information, see Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials or sign-up information
  • Location data
  • Health or medical data
  • Any data stored on external servers controlled by us

3. How We Use Information

  • Local data is used solely to provide App functionality (saving your progress and preferences)
  • AdMob data is used by Google to serve relevant advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics data is used to improve the App's design and performance

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with any third parties. We do not transmit any data from your device to third-party services.

Our App includes third-party SDKs (Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics) that operate directly on your device. These services may independently collect certain device and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not control, access, or share in the data they collect.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the App. Third-party service data is retained according to their respective policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
  • Request deletion of any data we control (note: we control no server-side personal data)
  • Exercise rights regarding Google's data processing via Google's My Activity

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
